Membership Benefits

NP Membership:

  • Ideal for non-profit organizations.
  • No membership fee required.
  • Access to essential resources and networking opportunities.
  • Tailored support for the specific needs of non-profit entities.

Bronze Membership:

  • Entry-level membership tier.
  • Affordable membership fee.
  • Basic benefits such as access to events and limited promotional opportunities.
  • Suitable for small businesses or startups looking to establish a presence within the community.

Silver Membership:

  • Mid-tier membership option.
  • Moderate membership fee with increased benefits.
  • Enhanced networking opportunities with industry leaders.
  • Additional perks such as discounted event tickets, advertising placements, and exclusive training sessions.

Gold Membership:

  • Premium membership level offering top-tier benefits.
  • Higher membership fee justified by extensive perks.
  • Prominent visibility through featured advertising spots on the organization's website and promotional materials.
  • Personalized support from Chamber of Commerce staff for business growth and development strategies.
